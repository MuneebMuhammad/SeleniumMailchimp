Custom Domain Setup

To get started, please ensure you have administrative access to your domain provider.

1. In another window, open your domain’s DNS admin settings.
2. Copy your Personal Benchmark Domain above and paste it as the new DNS Record value for the domain you’d like to connect.
3. Enter the domain you’ve updated in the Custom Domain Address field below.
4. Click Verify Domain to confirm the connection is successful.

Changes to your domain’s DNS Record may take up to 24 hours to update. Learn More
